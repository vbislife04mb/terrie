About Me

Hi my name is Terrie Franco and I want to be your pet sitter. I have a dog, July who is a mixed breed of Blue Heeler and Australian Shepherd and three cats, Snickers, Morgan, and Megan. I love animals of all sizes and personalities, especially dogs, cats, and horses. I was raised with animals (dogs, cats, horses, rabbits).

I am pet first aid certified and insured. Do your pets need medicine? I can give pills as well as shots. I believe pet therapy is the best therapy there is for people. I am dependable and trustworthy. I will love and care for you “family” members while you are away on vacation while watching your home as well. Your pets and I can take relaxing walks. I will give your pet love and care while you are away.
